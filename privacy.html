Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026 · quickutility.me

1. Introduction

Welcome to QuickUtility ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website at quickutility.me.

2. Information We Collect

QuickUtility does not collect, store, or sell personal information. All tools run entirely in your browser. No data you enter into our tools is sent to our servers.

3. Cookies & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense may use cookies to show relevant ads based on your browsing activity. We do not use tracking cookies ourselves. You can opt out of personalized ads by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense — advertising (google.com/adsense)
  • Firebase Authentication — optional user accounts (firebase.google.com)
  • Cloudflare — speed test and web analytics (cloudflare.com)
  • QR Server API — QR code generation (goqr.me)
  • ipapi.co — IP address lookup
  • open.er-api.com — currency exchange rates

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

5. User Accounts

Creating an account on QuickUtility is optional. If you create an account, we collect your email address and display name through Firebase Authentication. This information is used only to identify your account and is not shared with third parties.

6. Log Data

Like most websites, we may receive standard log data including your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This is used only for security and performance monitoring via Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is privacy-friendly and does not use cookies.

7. Children's Privacy

QuickUtility is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@quickutility.me
