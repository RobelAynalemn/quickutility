Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026 · quickutility.me

QuickUtility does not collect, store, or sell personal information. All tools run entirely in your browser. No data you enter into our tools is sent to our servers.

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements, which may use cookies to show relevant ads. We do not use tracking cookies ourselves. You can opt out of personalized ads by visiting Google's ad settings.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services: Google AdSense (advertising), Cloudflare (speed test backend), and QR Server API (QR code generation). These services have their own privacy policies.

Our IP lookup tool uses the ipapi.co service. When you use this tool, your IP address is sent to ipapi.co. We do not store this data.

QuickUtility is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
